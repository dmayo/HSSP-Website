Night Light Circuit!

To emphasize and apply your understanding of basic series and parallel circuits, you will be creating an automatic nightlight using a small solderless breadboard, LEDs, and a photoresistor. Pre-cut wires and resistors will also be provided. To ensure that everyone understands how their project will work, we will ask that you submit a schematic of your proposed nightlight circuit by a date which we will specify one week in advance. There is no right or wrong implementation of the nightlight so long as it is automatic, meaning it turns itself ononly in darkness, so creativity will be highly encouraged (surprise us, or ask us for ideas!). We will provide a steady 5-volt DC power source for the nightlight in class; this should be kept in mind as you design the circuit. Outside of class, you will also be able to power the nightlight using three AA or AAA batteries.
